About The Firm

Over 21 Years of Professional Excellence

Trusted Experts in Taxation & Compliance

M/s Meenu Garg & Co is a Chartered Accountancy firm with more than 21 years of professional experience in taxation, GST consultancy, audit and litigation.

She is empanelled as a Peer Reviewer with the Peer Review Board of ICAI and actively undertakes peer review assignments, contributing to the enhancement of professional quality and compliance within the accounting profession.

The firm has successfully represented clients before various tax authorities and appellate forums and is known for timely service, transparency and personalized guidance.

Her core areas of expertise include statutory audits of Cooperative Societies, Cooperative Banks, schools, non-profit organizations and various industrial establishments. She has also conducted audits of business entities with turnovers exceeding ₹5 crore.
